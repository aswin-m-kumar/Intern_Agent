About InternAgent

                    
InternAgent is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to extract and synthesize internship opportunities from any valid URL.

                    
By leveraging large language models, it seamlessly generates marketing materials such as poster content and WhatsApp captions, saving users hours of manual processing.
